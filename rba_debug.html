						Senior officers of the Reserve Bank give speeches and participate in panel discussions on a
						broad range of topics related to its role and functions. These are part of the Bank's efforts
						to promote understanding of its decision-making and facilitate accountability to accompany its
						operational independence.
					

					

						Request for RBA speakers can be submitted online via Event invitations form.
						For more
						information, please see Arrangements for Public Speaking Engagements.
